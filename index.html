CAPABILITIES

Built for academic-grade rigor

Everything you need to separate real, supported citations from fabricated ones.

neurology

AI claim-level support checking

Goes beyond "does it exist." Our model reads the cited PDF and confirms the source actually backs the specific claim it's attached to — catching misrepresented and over-stated citations.

database

Multi-database lookup

Cross-checks every reference against CrossRef, PubMed, OpenAlex, DataCite, Google Books, and the Internet Archive for an authoritative existence verdict.
